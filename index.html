Attended my first rehearsal for the SF Gay Men's Chorus. After singing with the NYC Gay Men's Chorus for over 5 years, I was so impressed with this group. Tim Selig made the rehearsal so much fun and everyone was so great welcoming me to the group. As a potential new member, they also gave all of us newbies special time to ask questions and to help us get comfortable with the audition process. These guys sound amazing and really seem like an extended family. There's even a Lollipop Guild - so what could be better than that. I'm keeping my fingers crossed that get to become a part of this amazing Group of guys!

-John, New York, NY
